My family is pretty awesome (to me anyway). They are so much fun and bring me a lot of happiness. I love spending time with them and making memories. We are all very different, but we all support eachother and help eachother be our best! Here is a little more about each of them.

Travis

Travis was born in St. Louis, MO and he grew up in Alton, IL. He attended Alton High and played football and wrestled. He has 3 sisters and 1 brother. He is the oldest. As a teenager he attended Greater Alton Church which planted The Crossings Church in Wentzville, MO.

Travis moved to Wentzville for the church plant in 2007, when I met him at Lindenwood University. We were married in 2009. August 8, 2019 will be our 10 year anniversary!

Lyla

Lyla Grae was born on July 12, 2011 at Missouri Baptist Hospital. She was 7 lbs. 5 oz. and 21.5 inches. She has super curly brown hair and really long eyelashes!

Lyla has always been really tall. She considers herself a tomboy. She loves to read, play any sports, play outside, and she is learning

guitar!

Ayven

Ayven Asher was born on September 30, 2017 at Missouri Baptist Hospital. She was 8 lbs. 1 oz. and 21.5 inches. She has curly blonde hair and long eyelashes like her sister. We call her Avie.

Avie loves to talk, play, and hang out with Lyla. Lyla has taught her so much, she reads with her and teaches her new words. Avie is adventurous and loves to explore things. She also has several food allergies, which has been a new adjustment for us, but she handles it so well!
